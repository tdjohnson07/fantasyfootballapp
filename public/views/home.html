Welcome to Fantasy Football Draft App

Here you can select which activity you would like to do. You can either choose to do an auction draft or a traditional snake draft, or you can view previous drafts that you have saved.

In an auction draft you bid on players then place them on the selected team.

In Snake Draft teams draft in order in one round then reverse the order the next round. There is also a clock option that limits the time you have to make your picks.

Here you can view the results of your previous drafts that you have saved.
